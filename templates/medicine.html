Chicken pox - Medicine

1)If you have a fever, your doctor may recommend acetaminophen or ibuprofen.

2)If you appear dehydrated and are unable to drink fluids, your doctor may recommend IV fluids either in an emergency room or as a hospitalized patient.

3)Secondary bacterial skin infections may be treated with antibiotics. Because a virus causes chickenpox, no antibiotic can cure the disease.

4)Neonatal VZV infection may be treated with VZIG (varicella zoster immune globulin) -- a form of highly concentrated anti-VZV gamma globulin.

5)Consult a doctor for medical advice
